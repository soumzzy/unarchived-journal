unarchived

What is this

A local-first journal. Write in your browser. Everything stays on your machine. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

Writing

Type freely. Word count updates as you write. Auto-saves every time you pause. Cmd/Ctrl+S to manually save.

Highlighting

Select text to highlight it. Choose a colour, then pick text-only, background, or both. Highlights persist and export correctly.

Timer

Click the timer button to show/hide. Leave minutes blank for a stopwatch. Enter a number for countdown. Session duration is recorded.

Tools

Click "tools" to access prompt templates. System, Context, Instructions, Example, Query, Reference—structured sections to draft detailed prompts.

Entries

Click the menu (☰) to see all your entries. Click one to open it. Right-click to delete. Cmd/Ctrl+N for a new entry.

Export

Click export to download an entry as PDF, Markdown, or HTML. Perfect for moving things elsewhere while keeping your archives local.

Theme

Click the moon icon (◐) to toggle dark/light mode. Your preference sticks around.
